Teacher Center Plan and Present
The Plan and Present window displays instructional content, gives you access to ready-to-use lessons, and allows you to customize pre-built lessons with your own resources.
Teacher Center Plan and Present
Lesson Planning tools allow you to add specific lessons to your teaching calendar, customize & create lesson plans for the chapter and lessons, and print lessons to PDF for offline learning. 
The Presentation button accesses presentations that walk you through the lesson.
Tips, strategies and suggestions for implementing the lesson are displayed in the Engage field.  
Lesson-reinforcing resources are located on the right side of the screen. 
Click Engage to close the Engage field.
All the folders in this lesson are now displayed. Click Engagement Toolbox. 
The Engagement Toolbox field displays lesson strategies, exercises, activities, and resources.
Click a resource’s Gear Icon to display a menu of options.
Teacher Center Assign
Click Assign this Resource.
The Assignment Name and Instructions field allows you to name an assignment and provide instructions. If you are assigning LearnSmart, you can include instructions about what students will Read, Practice, or Recharge(review). You can also identify specific students for assignments and choose when assignments will expire. 
Lesson strategies include critical thinking using a virtual lab, skill practice through learning exercises, writing support activities, and developing concepts through scaffolding and questions.
The Make an Assignment screen displays with the resource located on the right-hand side of the page in the Attach Resources and Content field.
To add a resource to a homework assignment, click Edit in the Attach Resources and Content field.
Select students that will receive assignments and send out student assignment notifications in this area.
The Edit Resources and Content screen displays. Click the Add button under a resource to add it to the assignment. In this example, we are clicking Add to attach LearnSmart, an adaptive teaching and learning tool, to the assignment.

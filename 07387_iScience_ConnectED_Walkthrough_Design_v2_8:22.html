Teacher Center Assign
Click on “Assign this Resource."
Using the fields and formatting tools, you can give the assignment a title and instructions. If you are using LearnSmart, you can be specific about what students will Read, Practice, or Recharge(review). You can also decide who gets what assignments and if you want it to expire at a certain time. 
Strategies can include Critical Thinking through a Virtual Lab, Skill Practice through visual learning exercises, Writing Support activities, and Developing Concepts through scaffolding and asking questions.
